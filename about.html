About FIS/USSA Points Calculator

FIS/USSA Points Calculator is a free tool designed to help racers, parents, and coaches calculate FIS and USSA points for alpine ski races as they happen.

Features

  • Calculate FIS and USSA points instantly from live race data
  • Supports FIS livetiming for races on the FIS app
  • Supports Vola Livetiming and standard Livetiming for races in the US
  • Predictive scoring for first runs in SL/GS events

Feel free to contact me if you have any requests for features or feedback about the site.
